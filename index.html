Claire Lally, Dog Trainer

My name is Claire and I am a dog trainer and behaviour consultant who works with families on a one-to-one and group basis helping them to understand their dogs and together build a happy, successful relationship. I have extensive experience having worked with everything from new born puppies, to over the top adolescents to adult dogs with very complex behavioural issues. In my previous role with the Dublin SPCA I was responsible for overseeing the behavioural care of 100 plus dogs in the Rehoming Centre at any one time along with nearly 5,000 dogs that had been rehomed since I started working there in 2012. When working with any dog my approach is to always strive to understand why they behave the way they do and through this help shape them into the well-rounded pets we all wish to have.

Group Lesson

Group lesson with 4-5 puppies or adults. Lessons include basic obidence and discussions on some common problem behaviours. Homework will be given and a check up will be had two weeks after the class.

Single 1 on 1 Class

Single 1 on 1 Class, covering basic obidence and a discusson on particular problem behaviours. General training plan is used and homework is given at the end of the class

House Visit

Single 1 on 1 Class, covering basic obidence and problem behaviours, ending with a personalised training plan and regular follow-ups with altered training plan as training progresses.
